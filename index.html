How to get your Discord token
1
Open Discord in your browser (discord.com/app) or desktop app
2
Open DevTools by pressing F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I (Windows) / Cmd+Option+I (Mac)
3
Go to the Network tab and check Preserve log
4
Type @me in the filter box, then refresh the page (F5)
5
Click on any request that appears (e.g., @me or library)
6
Go to Headers tab, scroll down to Request Headers
7
Find authorization: and copy the token value (starts with letters/numbers)
Warning: Never share your token with anyone! It gives full access to your account.
